06/About

Hi, I'm Jenny. I just finished my dual Bachelor's in IT Security at SAP and the University of Applied Sciences Darmstadt (h_da), where I wrote my thesis on CodeAct, an AI agent approach that executes actions through code instead of individual JSON based tool calls.

Next, I'm continuing with a dual Master's through the SAP STAR program in the AI Agent Foundation, while studying deep learning, large language models, and other exciting AI topics at Technische Universität Darmstadt over the next two years.

With this background in both AI and security, I look forward to contributing to the development of AI agents that are not only powerful but also secure.
